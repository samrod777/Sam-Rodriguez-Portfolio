About Me

Sam is a Product Development Director with full-stack web development skills and entrepreneurial ambitions. During his 10 plus years in the Government and Nonprofit industry, he has worked in multi-level relations management, product development projects from begining to end, and technical documentation.

Sam has a pilot's license, as well as an instrument ground instructor certificate, and enjoys sightseeing, taking aerial photographs, and writing trip reports.

He holds a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Ingeneering from San Jose State University and a Certificate of Business Administration from Darden School of Business. In February 2021, he will be completing an immersive, certificate full-stack web development bootcamp from the University of Washington.
