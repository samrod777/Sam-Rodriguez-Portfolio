About Me

Hi, my name is Sam Rodriguez, I am currently the Product Development Director at Lighthouse for the Blind, Inc. I also have full-stack web development skills and entrepreneurial ambitions. I earned a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from San Jose State University and a Certificate of Business Administration from Darden School of Business. In February 2021, I completed an immersive, certificate full-stack web development Bootcamp from the University of Washington.
